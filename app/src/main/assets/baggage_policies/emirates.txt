Emirates Baggage Policy

Checked Baggage Allowance:
Emirates offers generous baggage allowances across all travel classes.

Economy Class:
Africa, Europe, and Middle East Routes:
- Number of bags: 1 checked bag
- Weight limit: 23 kg (50 lbs) per bag
- Dimensions: Maximum 150 cm (59 inches) total (length + width + height)

Americas, Asia, and Australia Routes:
- Number of bags: 2 checked bags
- Weight limit: 23 kg (50 lbs) per bag
- Dimensions: Maximum 150 cm (59 inches) total per bag

Economy Special Fare:
- Number of bags: 1 checked bag
- Weight limit: 35 kg (77 lbs)
- Dimensions: Maximum 150 cm (59 inches) total

Premium Economy Class:
- Number of bags: 2 checked bags
- Weight limit: 25 kg (55 lbs) per bag
- Dimensions: Maximum 150 cm (59 inches) total per bag

Business Class:
- Number of bags: 2 checked bags
- Weight limit: 32 kg (70 lbs) per bag
- Dimensions: Maximum 150 cm (59 inches) total per bag

First Class:
- Number of bags: 2 checked bags
- Weight limit: 32 kg (70 lbs) per bag
- Dimensions: Maximum 150 cm (59 inches) total per bag
- Note: First Class passengers can combine allowance for fewer, heavier bags

Carry-On Baggage:
Economy Class:
- 1 cabin bag: Maximum 7 kg (15 lbs), dimensions 55 x 38 x 20 cm
- 1 personal item: Handbag, laptop bag, or small backpack

Premium Economy, Business, and First Class:
- 1 cabin bag: Maximum 7 kg (15 lbs), dimensions 55 x 38 x 20 cm
- 1 briefcase or handbag: Maximum 7 kg (15 lbs), dimensions 45 x 35 x 20 cm

Excess Baggage Fees:
- Additional bag: USD $150-$400 depending on route
- Overweight bag (23-32 kg): USD $50-$150
- Overweight bag (over 32 kg): Must be split into multiple bags or shipped as cargo
- Oversized bag (over 150 cm): USD $50-$100

Special Items:
- Sports equipment: Golf clubs, skis, and diving equipment can be included in baggage allowance
- Musical instruments: Can be carried in cabin if within size/weight limits, or purchased seat
- Infant equipment: Strollers, car seats, and travel cots are free and do not count toward baggage allowance

Skywards Members (Frequent Flyer Program):
- Blue (Basic membership): Standard allowance
- Silver: +1 additional bag (23 kg)
- Gold: +1 additional bag (32 kg)
- Platinum: +1 additional bag (32 kg)

Infants (Under 2 years):
- 1 checked bag: 10 kg (22 lbs)
- 1 collapsible stroller or car seat (free, doesn't count toward allowance)

Students:
- Additional 10 kg allowance on select routes (must present valid student ID)

Special Allowances:
- Passengers traveling to/from certain destinations in Africa may have different allowances
- Codeshare flights may have different baggage policies
- Always verify your specific ticket's baggage allowance

Note: Emirates is known for its generous baggage policies compared to many other international carriers.
