Southwest Airlines Baggage Policy

Checked Baggage Allowance:
Southwest Airlines is famous for its "Bags Fly Free" policy, offering complimentary checked baggage.

Standard Checked Baggage (FREE):
- Number of bags: 2 checked bags FREE
- Weight limit: 50 lbs (23 kg) per bag
- Dimensions: Maximum 62 inches (158 cm) total (length + width + height)

This is Southwest's signature policy - the first two checked bags are always free for every passenger!

Third and Additional Bags:
- Third bag and beyond: $75 per bag
- Weight limit: 50 lbs (23 kg) per bag
- Dimensions: Maximum 62 inches (158 cm) total

Overweight Baggage:
- Bags 51-100 lbs (23-45 kg): $75 per bag
- Bags over 100 lbs (45 kg): Not accepted as checked baggage

Oversized Baggage:
- Bags 63-80 inches (159-203 cm): $75 per bag
- Bags over 80 inches (203 cm): Not accepted as checked baggage

Carry-On Baggage (FREE):
- 1 carry-on bag: Maximum dimensions 10 x 16 x 24 inches (24 x 40 x 60 cm)
- 1 personal item: Purse, briefcase, laptop bag, or small backpack that fits under seat
- No weight limit for carry-on items, but must be able to lift into overhead bin

Special Items:
Sports Equipment (First two items count as free checked bags):
- Golf bags: Free (counts as 1 of 2 free bags)
- Skis and snowboards: Free (counts as 1 of 2 free bags)
- Surfboards: Free (counts as 1 of 2 free bags)
- Bicycles: Free (counts as 1 of 2 free bags, must be in proper container)

Musical Instruments:
- Small instruments: Can be carried on if they fit in overhead bin or under seat
- Large instruments: Can purchase additional seat or check as baggage

Infant and Child Items (FREE):
- Strollers: Free, doesn't count toward baggage allowance
- Car seats: Free, doesn't count toward baggage allowance
- Diaper bags: Free, doesn't count toward personal item

Military Baggage:
Active duty military personnel:
- 3 free checked bags (instead of 2)
- Weight limit: 100 lbs per bag
- Must present military ID

Rapid Rewards Members:
- A-List and A-List Preferred: Same baggage policy (2 free bags)
- Companion Pass: Companion also gets 2 free checked bags

Unaccompanied Minors:
- Same baggage allowance: 2 free checked bags

Important Notes:
- Southwest's "Bags Fly Free" policy applies to all fare types
- This policy makes Southwest very competitive for families and travelers with lots of luggage
- No change fees on tickets (separate from baggage policy, but worth noting)
- Baggage fees apply per person, per direction of travel
- Some items like hazardous materials are prohibited

Comparison Advantage:
While most U.S. airlines charge $30-$35 for the first checked bag and $40-$45 for the second, Southwest's free baggage policy can save a family of four up to $240 on a round-trip flight.
