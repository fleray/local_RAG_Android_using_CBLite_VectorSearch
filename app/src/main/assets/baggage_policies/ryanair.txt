Ryanair Baggage Policy

Checked Baggage Allowance:
Ryanair is a low-cost carrier with a pay-for-baggage model. Checked baggage is not included in the standard fare.

Standard Fare (No Checked Baggage Included):
- Number of bags: 0 bags included
- Must purchase checked baggage separately

Checked Baggage Options (Must be purchased):
20kg Checked Bag:
- Weight limit: 20 kg (44 lbs)
- Dimensions: Maximum 81 x 119 x 119 cm
- Price: €25-€60 depending on route and booking time (cheaper when booked online in advance)

Additional Bags:
- Up to 3 bags total per passenger
- Each additional bag: Same price as first bag
- Maximum weight per bag: 20 kg

Overweight Baggage:
- Bags over 20 kg are not accepted
- Passengers must repack or pay for additional bag

Carry-On Baggage:
Small Bag (Free for all passengers):
- Dimensions: 40 x 20 x 25 cm
- Must fit under the seat in front
- Examples: Small backpack, handbag, laptop bag

Priority Boarding (Additional Purchase Required):
- 1 small bag (40 x 20 x 25 cm) - free
- 1 cabin bag (55 x 40 x 20 cm, maximum 10 kg) - only with Priority Boarding
- Priority Boarding cost: €6-€36 depending on route and booking time

Non-Priority Passengers:
- Only 1 small bag allowed in cabin
- Larger cabin bags will be placed in hold (free of charge but subject to availability)

Excess Baggage Fees:
- Checked bag purchased at airport: €70 per bag (much more expensive than online)
- Cabin bag violation: €25-€50 if bag doesn't meet size requirements
- Overweight bag: Not accepted, must repack

Special Items:
- Sports equipment: €45-€60 per item (golf clubs, skis, etc.)
- Musical instruments: Must fit in cabin bag allowance or be checked as sports equipment
- Infant equipment: 1 stroller and 1 car seat free of charge

Important Notes:
- Ryanair has very strict baggage policies
- Bags are frequently checked at the gate
- It's much cheaper to purchase baggage allowance online before your flight
- No free checked baggage on any fare type
- Baggage fees are per flight, not per round trip
