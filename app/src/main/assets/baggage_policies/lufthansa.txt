Lufthansa Baggage Policy

Checked Baggage Allowance:
Lufthansa passengers are entitled to different baggage allowances depending on their travel class and route.

Economy Class:
- Number of bags: 1 checked bag on intercontinental flights, 0 on European flights (Light fare)
- Weight limit: 23 kg (50 lbs) per bag
- Dimensions: Maximum 158 cm (62 inches) total (length + width + height)
- Additional bags can be purchased for a fee

Premium Economy Class:
- Number of bags: 2 checked bags
- Weight limit: 23 kg (50 lbs) per bag
- Dimensions: Maximum 158 cm (62 inches) total per bag

Business Class:
- Number of bags: 2 checked bags
- Weight limit: 32 kg (70 lbs) per bag
- Dimensions: Maximum 158 cm (62 inches) total per bag

First Class:
- Number of bags: 3 checked bags
- Weight limit: 32 kg (70 lbs) per bag
- Dimensions: Maximum 158 cm (62 inches) total per bag

Carry-On Baggage:
Economy and Premium Economy:
- 1 carry-on bag: Maximum 8 kg (17 lbs), dimensions 55 x 40 x 23 cm
- 1 personal item: Handbag, laptop bag, or small backpack

Business and First Class:
- 2 carry-on bags: Each maximum 8 kg (17 lbs), dimensions 55 x 40 x 23 cm
- 1 personal item

Excess Baggage Fees:
- Additional bag (Economy): €70-€150 depending on route
- Additional bag (Premium Economy/Business): €50-€100
- Overweight bag (23-32 kg): €50-€100
- Overweight bag (over 32 kg): Not accepted, must be shipped as cargo

Special Items:
- Sports equipment: May be included in baggage allowance or charged separately
- Musical instruments: Can be carried as cabin baggage if within size limits
- Infant equipment: Strollers and car seats are free of charge

Miles & More Members:
- Frequent Traveller: +1 additional bag
- Senator: +1 additional bag
- HON Circle: +1 additional bag

Note: Baggage policies may vary by route and ticket type. Always check your specific booking for exact allowances.
